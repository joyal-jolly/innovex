General Protocols

Important guidelines for all participants

Important Notice

All participants must read and adhere to these protocols. Non-compliance may lead to disqualification.

1

On 23rd January, spot registration starts at 9:00 AM. Both online registration and spot registration are available.

2

The competition is open to all UG, PG (engineering, medical), and open university students with valid ID cards from recognized institutions.

3

Colloquium and Blind Coding are exclusive for school students only.

4

Participants should go through event guidelines and adhere to the rules and regulations.

5

Any number of teams/individuals from a campus are allowed to participate in the events.

6

All members of the team should be from the same institution/campus.

7

Students must ensure stable internet connectivity for mandatory games, as the college is not liable for any connectivity issues.

8

Any misbehavior from participants will lead to disqualification of the whole team from the event.

9

Decision made by judges and event heads will be considered as final.

10

Any doubts regarding the event must be cleared before the commencement of events.

11

Participants are advised to safeguard their belongings, as the organizers are not liable for any loss.

12

Fest ID tags will be issued to all the registrants from the registration counter.

13

Once registered, the registration fee will not be refunded.

14

College ID card / authorization letter should be produced at the time of registration.

Need Assistance?

For any queries regarding these protocols, please contact the event coordinators or visit the registration desk.
